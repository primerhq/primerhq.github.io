## What's in the cookbook

Each recipe is a complete, working solution you can build on your own instance.
Recipes follow one fixed shape so they are easy to scan and adapt:

- **Goal** - what you are building and why someone wants it.
- **Ingredients** - every feature and external dependency the recipe needs.
- **Walkthrough** - the ordered steps, with the exact requests that work.
- **Testing** - concrete inputs to exercise the result and what to expect.

Recipes are added here as they are built and verified end to end. The first
batch is in progress.
